## About Us

**Time 4 Action** is a Ljubljana-based company operating under [Creaglobe International](https://time-4-action.com). We manage all European logistics and brand operations for **PATRIK** and its affiliated brands in the windsurfing industry.

Alongside our core operations, we develop in-house **enterprise AI solutions** that streamline how our teams and partners interact with business data — from ERP systems and product catalogs to document management and customer communications.

## What We Build

Our internal engineering team develops tools that power day-to-day operations across the organization:

- **AI-Powered Business Assistant** — A conversational interface backed by large language models, enabling employees to query ERP data, manage emails, analyze documents, and access company knowledge through natural language.

- **Administration & Analytics** — Centralized dashboards for user management, access control, usage tracking, and cost analytics across all internal tools.

- **Partner Portal** — A B2B platform giving partners direct access to product catalogs with real-time pricing, stock levels, and configurable data exports.

- **Data Integration Pipelines** — Automated workflows that synchronize and enrich product data across multiple sources, preparing it for downstream systems and partner channels.
